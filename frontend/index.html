Get the app on your phone

1. Install the app

  1. Open this site in Chrome (you're probably here already).
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu in the top-right corner.
  3. Tap Add to Home screen (sometimes called Install app).
  4. Confirm — the CookBook icon appears on your home screen. Open it from there like any app.

2. Save recipes from other apps

  1. Found a recipe in Instagram, TikTok or Chrome? Tap Share.
  2. Pick Digital CookBook from the share list.
  3. Done — the recipe imports into your cookbook by itself. No internet or slow server? It's queued and sent automatically later.

  2. Tap Add Shortcut.
  3. When asked for an email, enter the same email you use in this app — that's how recipes reach your cookbook.

3. Save recipes from other apps

  1. Found a recipe in Instagram, TikTok or Safari? Tap Share.
  2. Pick the CookBook shortcut from the list.
  3. Wait for the confirmation alert — you never leave the app you're in.

How to use the app

➕ Add recipes

  1. Paste an Instagram or TikTok link into Add a recipe and tap Import — the recipe is read for you.
  2. Even easier: share straight from Instagram or TikTok to the CookBook (see How to install for the one-time setup).
  3. Or tap ✎ Or create one yourself to type a recipe in, with an optional photo.

🍳 Your recipes

  1. Tap a recipe card to open its ingredients and steps; tap again to close.
  2. ✎ edits a recipe, × deletes it, Watch Video opens the original post.
  3. ★ marks a favorite. 🔖 puts it on your Up Next list — things you plan to cook soon.

🏷 Tags & search

  1. Tap + on a recipe card to tag it (pick an existing tag or make a new one).
  2. Tap the tag pills at the top to filter your recipes — you can combine several. More ▾ shows all your tags.
  3. The search box finds recipes by title, ingredient or tag.

↗ Share with family and friends

  1. Tap Share on a recipe card and pick a friend — or ✉ Someone else… and type their email (they need to have used this app once).
  2. Recipes shared with you appear under Shared with me: Add copies the recipe into your cookbook, Dismiss hides the offer.
