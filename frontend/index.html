Get the app on your phone

1. Install the app

  1. Open this site in Chrome (you're probably here already).
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu in the top-right corner.
  3. Tap Add to Home screen (sometimes called Install app).
  4. Confirm — the CookBook icon appears on your home screen. Open it from there like any app.

2. Save recipes from other apps

  1. Found a recipe in Instagram, TikTok or Chrome? Tap Share.
  2. Pick Digital CookBook from the share list.
  3. Done — the recipe imports into your cookbook by itself. No internet or slow server? It's queued and sent automatically later.

1. Install the app

  1. Open this site in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button (the square with an arrow, at the bottom).
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen, then Add.
  4. The CookBook icon appears on your home screen. Open it from there like any app.

2. One-time setup for saving recipes

  1. Tap this link: Get the CookBook shortcut.
  2. Tap Add Shortcut.
  3. When asked for an email, enter the same email you use in this app — that's how recipes reach your cookbook.

3. Save recipes from other apps

  1. Found a recipe in Instagram, TikTok or Safari? Tap Share.
  2. Pick the CookBook shortcut from the list.
  3. Wait for the confirmation alert — you never leave the app you're in.
